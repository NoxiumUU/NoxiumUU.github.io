    The Story

    It was the year 2018 when the earth had its first contact with aliens. The alien race didn't seem friendly, and they started a war. More and more spaceships came and after a long battle, the earth had been conquered by these alien creatures. Many people died. Others ran away and stayed hidden, hoping the aliens would never find them. Now, 50 years later, the earth is ready to make its first comeback. From all survivors over the world, young humans were chosen and trained to become the last hope of the human race. Now their training is done, and they are ready to save the earth. Help these fighters conquer back the earth!

    The project

    Noxium will be a fighting game, which has the key characteristics of a mortal combat game. The theme of the game will be futuristic with a focus on an alien invasion.
