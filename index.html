    The Story

    IIt is the year 2018. Out of nowhere, hundreds of aliens appear all across the globe. They call themselves inhabitants of Noxium, and are claiming the Earth for themselves. The war has just started but entire cities have already been destroyed. The Noxians seem unstoppable. Luckily, humanity still has a chance. The only ones who can stop them are our four heroes. Follow their story as they first meet the aliens, and come together from all over the world to bring an end to the war. Only you can help these fighters conquer back the earth!

    The project

    Noxium will be a fighting game, which has the key characteristics of a mortal combat game. The theme of the game will be futuristic with a focus on an alien invasion.
